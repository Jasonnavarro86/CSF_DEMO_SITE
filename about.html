ABOUT

An Information Technology Solutions Company

Founded in 2002 by Curtis Freeman, a Service Disabled Veteran of the U.S. Air Force, CSF Technologies, Inc. is an Information Technology Solutions Company. CSF delivers proactive management solutions and expert resources to support critical business technologies with detailed experience in IT Infrastructure Support and Systems Engineering. Committed to delivering innovative, agile, timely solutions that are cost efficient and effective.

CSF implements and supports managed solutions that include Help Desk, Audio/Visual and Telecom Equipment and Services, Network Operations and Database Administration. CSF is a trusted Value Added Reseller for numerous leading technology providers.

CSF is SBA certified 8(a) Small Disadvantages Business, HUBZone and CVE Verified Service Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business. CSF is a privately held corporation located in Camden, New Jersey.
‍
CSF has over 10 years’ experience in providing services and products to a primarily Federal Government customer base, but also supports Local Government and commercial enterprises.
