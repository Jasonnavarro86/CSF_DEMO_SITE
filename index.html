CSF Technologies

A Full-Scale Provider of IT Products...

CSF Technologies is a Full-Scale Provider of IT Products and Related Services to the Federal Government. Our technical experience and understanding of IT Infrastructure services and security allows us to provide the best value purchasing opportunities to our Federal agency customers.

We are a Prime Contractor and Subcontractor on GWACs, and IDIQs, designed to provide Information Technology products and services directly tofederal agencies. We also service local government, educational institutions and commercial companies.

CSF Technologies is a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB), Economically Disadvantaged HUBZone and 8(a) Certified Disadvantaged Small Business. We are ISO 9001:2015 Certified and all operations fall under our quality management system.
